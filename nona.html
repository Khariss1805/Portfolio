Nona Antojos


The project aimed to design a mobile-optimized web menu for Nona Antojos, a vibrant catering company renowned for its diverse and delectable offerings. The primary goal was to enhance the user experience by creating a seamless and visually appealing interface that allows customers to easily navigate and explore Nona Antojos’ menu on mobile devices.



Our goal was to address the challenge of time-constrained customers who need to quickly access and understand the menu offerings of Nona Antojos. Many potential clients are busy and require an efficient way to browse through the menu without spending excessive time. The primary challenge was to design a mobile web page that delivers a clear, concise, and visually appealing menu presentation, allowing users to quickly find and select their desired items. By tackling this issue, we aimed to enhance user satisfaction and streamline the ordering process, ultimately leading to a better customer experience and increased engagement with Nona Antojos.


To address the challenge of catering to time-constrained customers, we implemented a streamlined design process focused on enhancing usability and accessibility. We conducted user research to understand the needs of busy clients and employed rapid prototyping to test and refine our concepts. The final deliverable will be a mobile web page featuring an intuitive layout with easily navigable sections, prominent menu categories, and quick-access options for popular items. This design approach ensures that customers can swiftly view and select their desired items, thus improving their overall experience and efficiency in ordering from Nona Antojos.


My role in this project involved leveraging UX design principles and content creation to ensure the menu is not only functional but also engaging. Through a user-centered design approach, we focused on simplifying the menu layout, optimizing load times, and ensuring a responsive design to meet the needs of today’s mobile users.
