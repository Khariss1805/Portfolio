La resiliencia de Irina


The Resilience of Irina addresses the critical issue of gender-based violence and the emotional toll it takes on individuals, particularly in vulnerable settings. Motivated by the need to raise awareness and foster empathy, the project aims to create an immersive virtual reality experience that resonates deeply with players.



We wanted to tackle the challenge of raising awareness about gender-based violence through an immersive and emotionally impactful experience, because we believe itâ€™s essential to foster empathy and understanding on this issue. This problem is particularly challenging since creating a virtual reality environment that deeply connects users with the character's emotional journey requires not only a strong narrative but also intuitive user interaction even more in Virtual Reality. The challenge also involved balancing educational content with an engaging and seamless gameplay experience, ensuring players remain emotionally invested throughout Irina's story and being really careful to not impact negative emotionally on the user.


To address the challenge of creating a deeply immersive and emotionally impactful VR experience for "La Resiliencia de Irina," we employed a comprehensive design process based on iterative sprints. Utilizing the design thinking methodology, we moved through the stages of empathize, define, ideate, prototype, and test. Our key deliverables included a richly developed narrative integrated into an engaging VR environment that vividly portrays Irina's emotional journey. This approach ensured a dynamic and interactive experience, enabling players to connect profoundly with the themes of resilience and personal growth presented in the game.


As the UX Designer and Content Creator, my role in the team involved crafting intuitive user experiences while developing compelling narratives that evoke strong emotional connections. The final output was a fully interactive VR game designed to educate and emotionally engage users in Irina's journey of resilience.
